Mission and Values

Our Mission

Our organization's mission is dedicated to improving the local living environment by implementing diverse initiatives that positively impact the community. From preserving nature and enhancing healthcare facilities to supporting premature babies and ensuring safety through vital equipment, our efforts contribute to creating a healthier, safer, and more nurturing environment for the residents of our city and country. By focusing on various aspects of community well-being, our organization plays a crucial role in making a lasting positive impact on the lives of those you serve.

"We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give."

Winston Churchill

Our Values

The values of our charity organizations encompass compassion, integrity, diversity, empowerment, collaboration, and accountability. We prioritize empathy and care for those in need, uphold honesty and ethical behavior, embrace diversity and inclusivity, aim to empower beneficiaries, work in partnership with others, and take responsibility for their actions and resources. These values guide our actions and decision-making processes, ensuring that we fulfill our mission and make a positive impact on society.

  • Compassion:
    We deeply care about the well-being of those in need and strive to understand and empathize with their challenges and struggles.
  • Integrity:
    We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our endeavors, ensuring that resources are used responsibly and effectively.
  • Diversity and Inclusivity:
    We embrace diversity and treat every individual with respect and dignity, regardless of their background, beliefs, or circumstances.
  • Empowerment:
    Instead of creating dependency, we aim to empower the communities we serve, equipping them with the tools and skills to improve their own lives.
  • Collaboration:
    We actively seek partnerships and cooperation with other stakeholders, including governments, NGOs, and local communities, to achieve greater collective impact.
  • Accountability:
    We take full responsibility for our actions and outcomes, ensuring that the trust of our donors and beneficiaries is well-placed.
  • Impact-Driven:
    We are committed to creating measurable and sustainable change in the lives of those we support, focusing on outcomes that lead to meaningful improvements.
  • Innovation:
    We embrace creativity and innovative solutions to address complex challenges and improve the effectiveness of our programs.
  • Stewardship:
    We recognize the importance of prudent management and stewardship of resources, maximizing their positive impact on our beneficiaries.
  • Volunteerism:
    We appreciate and value the dedication of our volunteers, recognizing their invaluable contributions to our mission.

Our Goals

Our charity organization's goals revolve around building a connected and proactive community, supporting external campaigns, improving local environments and living conditions, collaborating nationwide for greater impact, and encouraging values-based involvement in Kyustendil. These objectives collectively reflect your organization's dedication to making a meaningful difference at various levels, from the local community to the broader national context.

  1. Connect and Empower:
    To bring together proactive individuals locally, fostering a supportive community where members help and collaborate with one another.
  2. Support "Caps for the Future":
    To provide organizational, informational, and manpower support to the "Caps for the Future" campaign, contributing to its success and impact.
  3. Local Development:
    To identify and contribute to local causes, improving the environment and living conditions in the relevant settlement or district.
  4. Nationwide Collaboration:
    To collaborate with other clubs across Bulgaria, working collectively to enhance the environment and living conditions nationwide.
  5. Kyustendil Improvement:
    To actively engage in initiatives that aim to enhance living conditions in Kyustendil, making a positive impact in the local community.
  6. Values-Based Inclusion:
    To inspire like-minded individuals to join the club, fostering a sense of shared values and commitment to making a difference.
