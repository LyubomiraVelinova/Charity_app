History

Our journey of change

"Caps for the Future" was launched in October 2017. The main objective of the first campaign was of donating at least one CUVYOZ to a hospital in a less populated area. To everyone's surprise, we were able to donate the first one just FIVE weeks after our first campaign at the end of October. For 5 years, the campaign not only continues to gather strength and funds, but also its creator founded Clubs for the Future.

Since our humble beginnings in 2017, our charity project has grown into a powerful force of positive change, touching countless lives and making a difference in the community. It all started with a simple idea - collecting caps - but soon evolved into a profound mission to transform lives.
