Our work

Where we work

Starting from Sofia

Caps for Future began its remarkable journey in Sofia, the heart of Bulgaria. It all started with a passionate group of individuals who believed in making a difference for premature babies and their families. The initial efforts in Sofia laid the foundation for the impactful campaign that would soon spread across the nation. From the very first collection of caps, we witnessed the power of unity and compassion, igniting a spark that continues to fuel our mission today. Throughout the years, our team in Sofia has worked tirelessly to improve neonatology departments and support premature babies in every possible way. The relentless dedication of our volunteers and donors has led to a significant positive change in the city, and we're proud to say that our efforts have touched countless lives.

Expanding to Plovdiv and Beyond

As the impact of Caps for Future in Sofia grew, we knew it was time to share our mission with other cities in Bulgaria. Plovdiv became the next destination for our campaign, and the warm reception from the community was overwhelming. Plovdiv embraced our cause with open arms, and together, we continued our mission of providing hope and support to the littlest members of our society. Beyond Plovdiv, more cities across Bulgaria recognized the importance of our cause and joined our campaign. From bustling urban centers to serene towns, the movement for a brighter future spread like wildfire. Each new location brought fresh challenges and opportunities, but our unwavering commitment to our mission remained steadfast.

'Clubs for Future' Across Bulgaria

In 2022, when the idea of clubs for the future was born, 10 clubs were founded in different cities in Bulgaria. Each club works mainly on the territory of its city, but we stay connected with the other clubs and help each other all the time. After that clubs have been established in more than 40 cities across Bulgaria. These clubs serve as beacons of hope and positive change, rallying communities together to support our noble cause. Each 'Club for Future' represents a hub of compassion, where like-minded individuals come together to create a lasting impact in their respective regions. Through these clubs, we've fostered a culture of giving and caring, inspiring countless volunteers to take part in various charitable initiatives. From supporting premature babies to championing causes related to nature and elderly care in the mountains, our clubs have become driving forces for change in their communities. As the 'Clubs for Future' network continues to grow, so does our collective ability to create a brighter future for Bulgaria. Each club's journey is unique, driven by local needs and aspirations, but all share the common goal of making a meaningful difference in the lives of those we touch. Together, through our campaigns and 'Clubs for Future,' we are weaving a tapestry of compassion and love, uniting hearts across Bulgaria for a better tomorrow. Our reach may be vast, but our impact is deeply personal, bringing smiles to the faces of children, families, and all those who benefit from the warmth of our care.
