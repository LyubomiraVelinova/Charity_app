Caps for Future campaigns

"Caps for Future" campaigns are a powerful initiative that aims to collect plastic caps, rings, and handles of beverage bottles from all over Bulgaria. These plastic caps are then recycled into secondary raw materials, and with the funds generated, we purchase life-saving equipment like incubators for premature babies or children's neonatal ambulances. It's incredible to witness the impact of a small plastic cap in creating a big difference in the lives of our little ones.

Textile for Future campaigns

Through our Recycling for Future Campaigns, we aim to create a sustainable future by collecting recycled garbage and giving it new life. With a strong commitment to environmental conservation, we collect various recyclable items such as glass jars, including children's purees and bottles, as well as cans of infant formula and dog/cat food. Additionally, we focus on PET bottles, specifically those from soft drinks and beer, marked with "ALU," mainly sourced from mineral water, non-alcoholic beverages, and beer. The collected recyclables are sent for responsible recycling and converted into valuable resources. By doing so, we not only contribute to reducing waste and pollution but also generate funds that enable us to make a tangible difference in our communities. With the earnings from the Recycling for Future Campaigns, we invest in purchasing bins for our cities. These bins play a vital role in promoting proper waste disposal and encouraging more individuals to participate in recycling efforts.

Blood donation for Future campaigns

This is a transformative initiative that strives to make a life-saving impact on our community! With boundless enthusiasm and unwavering dedication, we champion the noble cause of free blood donation, providing a beacon of hope for those in need.

Our campaign extends its heartfelt invitation to all healthy individuals aged 18 to 60, empowering them to become heroes in their own right. By giving the gift of life through blood donation, you have the power to touch countless lives, offering a lifeline to patients battling acute blood loss, severe burns, and various life-threatening conditions.
