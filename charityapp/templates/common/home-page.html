Together we can do more

We gather active, proactive and constructive people to improve the environment in which we live. We work so that tomorrow we can live better than today!

Who We Are

We are children, teenagers, parents, women, men, grandparents, volunteers and dedicated people who want to live in wilder conditions and work hard to achieve it in the hole country Bulgaria. Club for the Future is created as a natural continuation of Caps for the Future, but concentrated locally.

What We Do

We run a range of high-impact campaigns and events that support premature babies, mountain victims, public awareness of first aid and nature. We started from a single cap, and now we are a whole society that is united under one cause - to participate in the improvement of living conditions in the country.

How To Involve

There are so many ways to make positive change for our country. Volunteer with us. Learn first aid. Don't forget to collect caps, jugs and plastic bottles. Donate blood. Make a donation to our organization.

Where We Work

The idea for Clubs for the Future comes from Lazar Radkov (the founder of "Caps for the Future") and the purpose of the clubs is to act locally in every city in the country where there is at least one person ready to start a club and work to improve the environment.

Our impact

Our main goal is to improve the living environment around us, we do it voluntarily and are guided by the motivation "Let tomorrow be better than today". Our organization play a crucial role in addressing societal challenges, promoting positive change, and making a significant impact on the lives of countless people, animals, and the environment.

Inspiring real Charity stories

There’s never been a more important time to invest in our future, than now.

During our first Blood Donation campaign, a member of our club donates blood. The day after, it turns out that an acquaintance of his is in URGENT need of blood. They start looking for a person with a suitable blood type, the information goes to the person from our club whose blood type turns out to be suitable. He immediately diverted the blood he had already donated to the hospital where his acquaintance was admitted. The man has his blood transfused and everything ends well.

There is no exercise better for the heart than reaching down and lifting people up.
