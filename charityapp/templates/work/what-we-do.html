Our work

What we do

Looking for ways to make the system work

We work for free and voluntarily, driven by the ambition to improve, if not the world, then at least the country in which we live. We have mandatory campaigns throughout the year, and the rest we come up with according to the needs of society. We are trying to fill those basic needs that have long been unmet in our society. Through dedicated efforts and a compassionate approach, we are committed to making a difference in the lives of those in need and contributing to a greener, more sustainable world. Our work is directed in several aspects. Here's an overview of our key initiatives:

Helping the Little Ones

Our organization recognizes the vulnerability of premature babies and the challenges faced by neonatology departments. We strive to make a difference by providing much-needed support to neonatology units. This includes donating crucial equipment, such as incubators and other specialized tools, to ensure that premature babies receive the best possible care.

Protecting Nature - Our Shared Home

Environmental preservation is a core pillar of our organization's mission. We actively champion initiatives to safeguard the environment, which is our collective responsibility. From tree planting campaigns to clean-up drives in local communities, we work tirelessly to protect and conserve nature for the benefit of future generations.

Providing Mountain Off-Road Ambulances

We understand the unique challenges faced by communities in remote mountainous areas. Our organization is dedicated to ensuring swift and reliable emergency medical services by providing mountain off-road ambulances. These specialized ambulances are equipped to navigate difficult terrains, reaching those in need during critical situations.

Empowering the Next Generation - First Aid Training for Children

We believe that empowering children with essential life skills is vital to their personal growth and community well-being. Our organization offers comprehensive first aid training programs designed specifically for young minds, preparing them to respond effectively in emergencies.

Championing Separate Waste Collection

Responsible waste management is a cornerstone of a healthier environment. We actively advocate and implement separate waste collection systems, inspiring communities to embrace recycling and eco-friendly practices.

Supporting Local Communities

At Club for Future, we prioritize collaboration with local partners and residents to address specific community needs. By working closely with the communities we serve, we ensure that our efforts have a meaningful and sustainable impact.

Inspiring Volunteerism

We believe in the power of collective action. Volunteerism is at the heart of our organization. Through engaging volunteer opportunities and community projects, we inspire individuals to actively participate in positive change.
